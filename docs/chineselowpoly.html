Chinese Low-Poly Stylized Environment

Description

This is a small stylized environment I made during the Spanish Game Jam in September 2020. The game concept was "duel" so the first thing that came to mind was a chinese environment, with its typical buildings, where two samurais will fight each other until one remains victorious.

All these assets were made inside Blender and the texturizing was made within Substance Painter.

Released: 2020
